Meet Our Experienced Team

Ryan - Senior Osteopath

InTune Clinic CEO

Ryan founded InTune Clinic in February 2014, driven by a passion for helping individuals achieve optimal health and wellness. With years of experience as an Osteopath in various clinics, he brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his practice.

Specializing in acute pain management, disk injuries, sciatica, and headaches, Ryan is well-versed in a wide array of advanced manual therapy techniques. His personalized approach ensures each patient receives tailored treatment, addressing their unique needs and promoting overall well-being.

Beyond his clinical work, Ryan is dedicated to education, frequently teaching and mentoring physiotherapy students on their MSK placements, sharing his expertise to shape the next generation of therapists.

Ryan's commitment to excellence is the cornerstone of InTune Clinic, where he fosters a supportive and welcoming environment for both patients and staff. His holistic approach to health and dedication to patient care set InTune Clinic apart as a premier destination for physiotherapy.

Outside of his professional life, Ryan enjoys spending quality time with his family. He is an avid fan of UFC, appreciating the discipline and dedication the sport entails. A lover of great stories, Ryan finds joy in films, books, TV shows, anime, and video games, always seeking new narratives to explore.

Amanda - Senior Osteopath

Pregnancy Specalist

Amanda graduated from the British College of Osteopathic Medicine with a Masters in Osteopathy in 2012. With over a decade of experience, she has honed her expertise in pregnancy pain management and women's health. Amanda is not only a dedicated practitioner but also an accomplished author. Her book, "Don’t Minimise," provides invaluable insights into pregnancy-related aches and pains, offering practical advice and support to expectant mothers.

At InTune Clinic, Amanda is known for her compassionate approach and her commitment to improving the well-being of her patients. She tailors her treatments to the unique needs of each individual, ensuring that every patient receives the highest standard of care.

In addition to her clinical work, Amanda is passionate about education and outreach. She creates engaging online content focused on women's health issues, striving to empower women with the knowledge they need to take control of their health. Through her videos, articles, and social media presence, she reaches a broad audience, providing advice and answering common health questions.

Outside of her professional life, Amanda enjoys exploring new destinations and cultures through her travels. Her adventures around the world enrich her life and bring a fresh perspective to her practice.

Ashleigh - Physiotherapist

Deep Tissue specalist

Ashleigh graduated from Northumbria University with a Masters in Physiotherapy in 2024. Throughout her academic journey, she gained extensive experience in a wide range of musculoskeletal (MSK) techniques and specialized care areas including elderly care, pelvic health, and neurological conditions. These skills were honed through various placements, providing her with a well-rounded foundation in physiotherapy.

Before joining InTune Clinic, Ashleigh was a sought-after Soft Tissue Therapist, known for her exceptional therapeutic skills and patient care. Her transition to a physiotherapist role at InTune Clinic has been seamless, allowing her to bring her expertise and compassionate approach to a broader range of patients.

Ashleigh's dedication to her profession is evident in her personalized treatment plans, which are tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient. Her goal is to promote optimal recovery and enhance the overall well-being of those under her care.

Outside of her professional commitments, Ashleigh leads an active lifestyle. She captains a netball team, showcasing her leadership and team spirit. Additionally, she enjoys spending time in the gym, as well as engaging in outdoor activities such as running, hiking, and camping. These hobbies not only keep her fit but also reflect her passion for health and wellness.

John - Physiotherapist

Elderly Care Specalist

John graduated from Northumbria University with a Bachelor's in Physiotherapy in 2020. Following his graduation, he gained valuable experience working as a Physiotherapist in the NHS, where he developed a deep understanding of elderly care and a wide variety of musculoskeletal (MSK) techniques.

In 2021, John joined InTune Clinic, bringing his extensive knowledge and compassionate care to the team. He is dedicated to providing personalized treatment plans that cater to the unique needs of each patient, with a particular focus on promoting mobility, reducing pain, and enhancing overall quality of life.

John's professional expertise is complemented by his warm and approachable demeanor, making him a favorite among patients. His ability to connect with individuals and understand their specific health concerns ensures that they receive the highest standard of care.

Outside of his professional life, John enjoys an active lifestyle. He loves walking his pet Labrador, Murphy, and participates in various sports and outdoor pursuits. Whether it's hiking, playing team sports, or exploring new trails, John's enthusiasm for physical activity is evident in his personal and professional endeavors.
