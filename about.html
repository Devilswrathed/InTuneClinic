Meet the team

Ryan - Senior Osteopath

InTune Clinic CEO

Ryan founded InTune Clinic in February 2014, driven by a passion for helping individuals achieve optimal health and wellness. With years of experience as an Osteopath in various clinics, he brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his practice.

Specializing in acute pain management, disk injuries, sciatica, and headaches, Ryan is well-versed in a wide array of advanced manual therapy techniques. His personalized approach ensures each patient receives tailored treatment, addressing their unique needs and promoting overall well-being.

Beyond his clinical work, Ryan is dedicated to education, frequently teaching and mentoring physiotherapy students on their MSK placements, sharing his expertise to shape the next generation of therapists.

Ryan's commitment to excellence is the cornerstone of InTune Clinic, where he fosters a supportive and welcoming environment for both patients and staff. His holistic approach to health and dedication to patient care set InTune Clinic apart as a premier destination for physiotherapy.

Outside of his professional life, Ryan enjoys spending quality time with his family. He is an avid fan of UFC, appreciating the discipline and dedication the sport entails. A lover of great stories, Ryan finds joy in films, books, TV shows, anime, and video games, always seeking new narratives to explore.

Amanda - Senior Osteopath

Amanda graduated from the British College of Osteopathic Medicine with a Masters in Osteopathy in 2012. With over a decade of experience, she has honed her expertise in pregnancy pain management and women's health. Amanda is not only a dedicated practitioner but also an accomplished author. Her book, "Donâ€™t Minimise," provides invaluable insights into pregnancy-related aches and pains, offering practical advice and support to expectant mothers.

At InTune Clinic, Amanda is known for her compassionate approach and her commitment to improving the well-being of her patients. She tailors her treatments to the unique needs of each individual, ensuring that every patient receives the highest standard of care.

In addition to her clinical work, Amanda is passionate about education and outreach. She creates engaging online content focused on women's health issues, striving to empower women with the knowledge they need to take control of their health. Through her videos, articles, and social media presence, she reaches a broad audience, providing advice and answering common health questions.

Outside of her professional life, Amanda enjoys exploring new destinations and cultures through her travels. Her adventures around the world enrich her life and bring a fresh perspective to her practice.

Amanda's dedication to her field and her patients, combined with her efforts to educate and empower women, make her an invaluable member of the InTune Clinic team.
